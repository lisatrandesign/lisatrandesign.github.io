Hey, I'm Lisa! I'm an aspiring Graphic Designer from Vancouver Island. I love Graphic Design because of the creative process and how it can communicate to the audience. I transitioned from a baker to a designer in 2015 and will be graduating in 2019 with a Bachelor's in Graphic Design. I'm interested in almost all fields of design; from UI and motion graphics to packaging and illustration. I enjoy food (a lil' too much tbh), travelling/sightseeing, and naps.

For any business inquiries, please send me a message!
